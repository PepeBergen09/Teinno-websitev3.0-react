## Language Settings

### Switching Languages
1. Look for the language selector (usually in the top menu)
2. Click on your preferred language (ES/EN)
3. The entire website content will update
4. Your language preference is remembered

### Available Languages
- **Spanish (ES)**: Default language
- **English (EN)**: Full translation available
